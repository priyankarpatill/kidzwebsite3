Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

Kidz 'n' KidZZilla was inaugurated in 2023 by Mrs. Malathi Adari, the proud proprietor, with a vision to enhance children's engagement through fun-filled activities and interactive learning experiences.

Our mission is to create a joyful environment where kids can explore, learn, and grow through play, creativity, and various educational activities. We aim to foster children's social, cognitive, and motor skills through a wide range of engaging programs and events.

KidZ ’n’ KidZZilla Indoor Play Area is a premier indoor kids' playground offering a clean, safe, and supervised environment filled with unique and exciting activities designed for fun and development. Children and parents alike can enjoy a bright, colorful, and friendly atmosphere with easy-listening music playing throughout the facility.

Our Philosophy

At Kidz n KidzZilla, we believe that every child has the potential to achieve great things.
