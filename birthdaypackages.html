📋 Rules & Regulations

Please review our policies to ensure a smooth and enjoyable celebration for everyone

  • Bookings must be made at least 7 days in advance
  • Parties are limited to 3-hour time slots
  • Additional time will be charged at ₹1,000 per hour
  • Setup and cleanup time is included in your booking

  • Maximum capacity is 50 guests including children
  • Additional guests beyond package limits will be charged ₹250 per person
  • At least one adult must remain present throughout the party
  • All children must be supervised by their parents/guardians

  • Outside food and beverages are not permitted
  • Special dietary requirements must be communicated 48 hours in advance
  • Alcohol is strictly prohibited on the premises
  • Cake cutting service is included in all packages

  • Confetti, glitter, and piñatas are not permitted
  • Decorations must not be attached to walls with tape or nails
  • All decorations must be removed at the end of the event
  • Any damage to property will incur repair/replacement charges

  • 50% advance payment required to confirm booking
  • Full payment due 7 days before the event
  • Cancellations 14+ days in advance: full refund
  • Cancellations 7-13 days in advance: 50% refund
  • Cancellations less than 7 days in advance: no refund
